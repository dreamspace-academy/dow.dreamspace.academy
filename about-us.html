DREAMS OF WOMEN IS A WOMEN-LED INITIATIVE FOR WOMEN


This initiative is a wing of the DreamSpace Academy. The aim is for women to serve other women to strengthen their capabilities and create a positive impact in their communities while challenging stereotypes of women..

Inspired by Dr APJ Abdul Kalam's view on 'Dreams', DOW transforms the dreams of young girls and women into their thoughts by developing their capabilities.

This initiative aims to support young girls and women in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics (STEAM). DOW firmly believes in a world where women equally contribute to the development of a community and a country.
