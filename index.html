What We Do

The initiative aims to support young girls and women in science, technology, engineering, mathematics and arts(STEAM). DOW firmly believes in a world where women equally contribute to the development of a community and a country.

Our Vision

To create a world where the dreams of women are nurtured by women and build a space where women have been equally embraced in all sectors, breaking any stigma against them in the global south.

Our Mission

Building the dreams of women by nurturing their dreams through challenged-based learning, grassroots innovation, and impact venture building.
