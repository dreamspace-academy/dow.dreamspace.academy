OUR STEAM

What We Do

This initiative aims to support young girls and women in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics (STEAM). DOW firmly believes in a world where women equally contribute to the development of a community and a country.

Science
Technology
Engineering
Arts
Maths

  • Science

    Empowering women in the science field is crucial for promoting diversity and equality in the workplace and for advancing scientific research and discovery.

    Highlighting the work of female scientists, and ensuring that their achievements are acknowledged and celebrated in the same way as those of their male colleagues. This can be requires a multifaceted approach that addresses the barriers that women face in the field, as well as the promotion of positive role models and the recognition of their contributions.

    Experimentation Research Innovation Agriculture

  • Technology

    Women have historically been underrepresented in the technology sector, and there is still a significant gender gap in the field. However, there are many initiatives and programs that aim to empower women and increase their participation in technology.

    Our organization offer mentorship programs for women in technology, connecting them with experienced mentors who can provide support and guidance.

    Communication Digital Marketing Software and hardware Development Data science application

  • Engineering

    Increase the visibility of successful women engineers. This can be done by highlighting the accomplishments of women engineers in the media, hosting networking events and conferences, and inviting women engineers to speak at industry events.

    Support and fund initiatives that focus on the recruitment, retention and advancement of women in engineering. This will help women engineers balance their work and family responsibilities, making it easier for them to continue working in the field.

    Building a model Improving a model Designing

  • Arts

    Promoting female artists: Museums and galleries can actively seek out and showcase the work of female artists, both established and emerging. This helps to increase visibility and recognition for women in the field.

    Encourage young girls to participate and pursue a career in art: by creating programs and events that introduce young girls to different art forms and mediums, providing access to resources and mentorship, and encouraging them to pursue a career in art.

    Poetry Writing Communications Video Story Telling Photography Videography Illustration

  • Maths

    It is important to recognize and celebrate the contributions of women in maths and analytics. This can include highlighting the achievements of female mathematicians and analysts, as well as promoting role models and success stories to inspire future generations of women in these fields.

    By providing women with the skills and resources they need to succeed, we can help to close the gender gap in maths and analytics.

    Collecting and Analyzing Data Mathematical Mapping Modelling
